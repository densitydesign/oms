We queried each subforum with the following queries:

Aufeminin.com

  • "césa"

For each thread extracted we computed the number of posts, the number of users involved and their average length.

visual exploration of forum structure and users behavior

The structure of the two different forums have been explored by comparing xxxxx posts through a Treemap diagram. The visualization exploits the measures extracted in the previous step, highlighting the hierarchical structure of the subforums and the different proportions of threads, posts and users. The network visualization has been chosen to highlight the participation of users in different subforums. The users have been divided in different categories depending on their presence in multiple forums, and dimensioned based on the number of comments posted.
