Harvesting the Relevant Webpages

We designed 9 different queries to collect the most relevant web-pages addressing the Family Planning. The different queries have been designed to address as much as possible “nuances” of the Family Planning issues: from the more medical ones to the more daily ones.
We qualified and tagged all the webpages accordingly to their contents obtaining three main webpages groups:

medical

relates to medical/public health information.

contentious

relates to sites that are more likely to show debate/controversy in the following areas: Risk & Benefit, Youth, Family Size, Law, Advocacy, Religion, Politics, Programme, Education, Development, Finances, Human Rights

natural family planning

relates to site that present information on natural family planning, they are worthwhile exploring as they often highlights the risk of artificial methods and might have religious affiliations.
