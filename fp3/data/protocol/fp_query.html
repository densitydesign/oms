harvesting the web corpus

We collected the first 100 Google.com results (in incognito mode) harvesting their URLs for each of the following queries: "family planning" + "sustainable development", "family planning" + "economic development", "family planning" + "population control", "family planning" + "population growth", "family planning" + "environmental development", "family planning" + "social development"

Rogers, Richard. "The Googlization Question, and the Inculpable Engine."

cleaning and tagging the webpages

We qualified and tagged all the webpages accordingly to their contents and excluding from the corpus pages not concerned with Family Planning (i.e. PDF, Videos, etc.). The resulting taxonomy is composed several groups as follow Policy , Demography, Economy, Rights, Health, Social, Advocacy, Sustainability, Women, Contraception, Culture, Religion, Youth, Unmet Need.

visual exploration of the aggregated results

We aggregated the web pages at the domain name level in order to have a better and quicker overview of the corpus contents and by using a visual faceted search we explored which queries produced more valid contents, which domain name is more relevant (i.e. .org or .gov; .com or .co.uk).
