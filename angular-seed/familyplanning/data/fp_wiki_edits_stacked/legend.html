Comparing normal and vandalism edits it is possible to see a higher activity in 2008-2009 on both articles, in particular for “Birth Control”. In this visualization is also evident the editors’ monitoring and control activity. After a vandalism edit, indeed, a revert action is often present, and the number of vandalism and revert edits is comparable for each month. Vandalisms are present through all the article history, highlighting how interest and debate on a controversial topic remains active also after a long time from the article’s creation and content sedimentation.
