Crawling and mapping the connections in the corpus

We followed the web-pages hyperlinks to discover other relevant URLs. After having aggregated the web pages at the domain name we created a network and its associated graph. Here the web sites are linked together if there is an hyperlink connecting their webpages.

The graph has been drawn using a force-vector algorithm. These algorithms works by arranging the nodes simulating a physical system where nodes repulse each other while arcs bounds them like springs.

In this kind of graph the distance is a signiﬁcant parameter to interpret the graph and it is possible to proceed to a quanti-qualitative analysis to discover two kind of clusters and nodes

the central nodes

Their centrality is a sign of high connectivity. They have the tendency to share a lot with an high number of others

the bridging nodes

They play a crucial role in allowing the circulation of “things” in the network. They connect actors with different “point of view” or “position” on the issue that the network tries to represent
