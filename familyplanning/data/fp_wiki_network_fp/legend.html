In the graph, nodes size is proportional to the number of authors. On Wikipedia, users usually focus on specific topics. If a user is active on an article, he will probably check related ones to avoid redundancies and keep a stylistic uniformity. The presence of the same group of editors in different articles can be a proxy of thematic affinity.
